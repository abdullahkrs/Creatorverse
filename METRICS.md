# Creatorverse Metrics

## North-star experiment

Measure whether a creator can turn an external audience into meaningful participation inside a realm.

## Activation funnel

1. Invite link opened.
2. Realm understood.
3. Role selected.
4. First mission completed.
5. Contribution visibly applied.
6. Result or invitation shared.

## Primary metrics

- Realm comprehension rate
- Role-selection rate
- First-mission completion rate
- Median time to first meaningful action
- Share-card generation rate
- Invite-to-activated-member conversion
- Day-two and day-seven activated-member retention
- Active-member rate per realm

## Fairness metrics

- Correlation between external follower count and realm rank
- Contribution concentration among top 1% of members
- Win rate by realm-size band
- Suspected automated activity rate

## Safety metrics

- Reports per 1,000 active members
- Confirmed off-platform harassment incidents
- Mission rejection rate by safety reason
- Median moderation response time
- Minor-safety escalations

## Milestone 0 targets

- 80% of test users explain the core concept correctly.
- 80% complete role selection without help.
- 70% complete the first mission in under 60 seconds.
- 60% say they would share the result or join a creator's realm.
