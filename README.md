# Creatorverse

Creatorverse is a mobile-first social strategy game where each creator builds a fictional digital realm with their audience. Followers become active members, complete short missions, develop the realm, form creator collaborations, and compete in safe seasonal events.

## Product promise

**Turn your audience into a living digital world.**

Creatorverse does not simulate real countries, politics, armies, borders, or real-world conflict. Everything happens inside a fictional digital universe and all competitive actions remain inside the product.

## MVP

The first playable version focuses on one complete loop:

1. A creator creates a realm.
2. Followers join through a shared link.
3. Members choose one of three roles: Builder, Explorer, or Guardian.
4. The creator launches a short mission from a safe template.
5. Member participation generates realm energy.
6. The realm unlocks a new district.
7. A result card is generated for the creator to share.

## Current stack

- Vite
- Vanilla JavaScript
- CSS
- Local mock data for the first interactive prototype
