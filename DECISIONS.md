# Creatorverse Decisions

## 2026-07-18 — Fictional digital universe only

Creatorverse will not use real countries, borders, flags, governments, political factions, wars, or geopolitical events. The product uses creator-led fictional realms and controlled in-product competition.

## 2026-07-18 — Audience activity over follower count

External follower count may be displayed as context later but will not directly determine realm strength. Progress and competition will use normalized active participation, retention, collaboration, and skill.

## 2026-07-18 — Existing social platforms remain the distribution layer

Creatorverse will not attempt to replace Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, Snapchat, or messaging apps. Creators share invite and result links externally; gameplay and outcomes remain inside Creatorverse.

## 2026-07-18 — Controlled missions before open creation

The MVP uses safe mission templates. Open-ended user-generated missions and unrestricted communication are deferred until moderation systems are proven.

## 2026-07-18 — Prototype before backend

The first milestone uses a lightweight Vite prototype and local state. Authentication, persistence, APIs, and real-time systems are deferred until usability and creator interest are validated.
